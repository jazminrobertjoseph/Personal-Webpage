About

My name is Robert Joseph Jazmin, a designer & developer from the Philippines, and this is a little story about my passion.

As a kid, I already had this special interest with computers. I always think it was cool and everytime I go to the library, I remember enjoying this big book about computer terms, only to boast the things that I've read to my friends afterwards. I also remember enjoying coding classes as it challenges me a lot in a logical sense along with the thrill of competing to finish first in the class.

Pursuant to my interests, I took Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. During that time, I learned that the world about computers is much more wider than I have ever thought. I also started focusing on programming, which later on came back to back with design. I also realized that writing these lines of codes are not just something you do to communicate with computer, it is an art.

Having finished my degree, I am ready to venture the path outside my boundaries once more. I may still be a bit of a greenhorn but I am always ready to take on challenges and keep on learning!
